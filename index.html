Large Language Models and Search (LLMs)

The engine behind most of what AI can do. Before anything else in this stack, you need one of these. Pick one, spend a week with it, and everything else will start to make sense.

🧠
What is a Large Language Model?
The engine behind almost every AI tool you will use.
A large language model has read a vast amount of the internet, books, articles, research and conversations. It has learned the patterns inside all of that information, and when you ask it a question, it predicts what the most useful response looks like, word by word.
Imagine you have read millions of books. Someone starts a sentence and based on everything you have ever read, you predict what word comes next. Then the next. Then the next. An LLM does exactly that but with patterns learned from the entire internet, at a speed no human could match. — Sarah
